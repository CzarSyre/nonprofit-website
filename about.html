Our Impact

Leading with faith, Tree Trunk Family Care is making a difference in the lives of families through our business trainings and skilling programs aimed at improving their economic well-being.

Our Core Values:

We aim to improve livelihoods of our community through compassionate and comprehensive care to families in need, fostering the core values of Respect, Teamwork, Faithfulness, Love, and Transparency.
