Volunteer Opportunities with Tree Trunk Family Care

Mentoring

Become a mentor to help guide and support individuals in their personal and professional growth. Share your expertise and experiences to inspire others.

Counseling

Offer emotional support and guidance to those in need. Utilize your communication skills to create a safe space for individuals to express their thoughts and feelings.

Skilling

Share your knowledge by teaching skills relevant to career development or personal improvement. Help individuals acquire new competencies that enhance their confidence and employability.

Community Outreach

Engage with the community to identify needs and align resources. Facilitate programs that support community development and foster relationships among residents.
