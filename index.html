Restoring Hope, preserving families and empowering children
Tree Trunk Family Care is a Christian-based organisation committed to strengthening families so that children can grow in love, faith, and dignity.

Mission

Providing social and economic support which is holistic in nature.

Vision

Improving the social and economic well being of vulnerable families so that children thrive in them.

Objective

To empower families through education, healthcare, and community engagement, creating a sustainable impact on their lives.

Train clients in business skills.
